Panther

Panther is a free and open source program that can encode your media files from one format to the other. It is very simple and easy to use.

Its features include the following:

·         You can convert your music files from one format to the other. It allows you simply to convert the file from one format to the other or you can even specify the minute details of the encoding process according to your preference.

·         You can reduce the size of your MP3 files without affect their quality. All you need to do is change the bitrate and Panther does the rest for you!

·         You can encode your video files from one format to the other as well. It includes the system of presets to speed up your encoding process by automatically providing the parameters. You can also create your own presets and also specify your own details. And Panther encodes your file!
